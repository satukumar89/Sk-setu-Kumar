About Me

I am a boy from a middle-class family. I dream of growing up. But life is very difficult. Such situations in life come and my heart dies. But I trust God a lot. I believe that God will not return me empty-handed from him. I love people a lot but I cannot speak well in front of people.

But I try to keep people very happy. If I have a relationship with someone, I do not let it be ruined, I maintain the relationship even if I am belittling myself. But I expect a little too much from people, so I suffer a lot. Whatever they say, I understand that life is not that easy. Sometimes you give up and move forward with small hopes again.
